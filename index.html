Bukas Palad Foundation of the Philippines, Inc. (BP) is a charitable organization dedicated to supporting the sustainable development, education, livelihood, and communication needs of various vulnerable groups in the Philippines. Here are the factors that contribute to my endorsement of this cause:


BP provides a wide range of support services to vulnerable populations in the Philippines, including sustainable development projects, education programs, livelihood opportunities, and effective information and communication strategies.


BP focuses on sustainable development, considering both immediate needs and the long-term well-being of communities and the environment.


As a non-profit and non-governmental organization, BP's primary goal is community service. All resources and funds collected directly support the organization's mission and benefit the intended recipients.
